THE TEENS CHURCH VISION

The Teens Church Vision To raise Boys of Valour and Girls of Virtue that will birth forth a new generation of end time soldiers for Christ. Strategic FocusTo reach every teenager who are committed to working with the Local church to raise lifelong followers of Jesus thereby leading as examples by their godliness in lifestyle, devotion to the Word of God and Prayer, having Passion for sharing the Love of Christ and committed to social involvement.


Core Values
  • Evangelism & Discipleship
  • Studying & Applying scriptures to daily living
  • Praying & fasting for the Salvation of lost teenagers; and empowerment of the Holy Spirit in all areas of their lives
  • Working in Partnership with the Local Church in Fulfilling the Mandate of the Commission
  • Committed to a life of integrity through Personal & Organizational accountability in lifestyle, Relationships, Finances and Responsibility.

    • Our Goals and Objectives
  • Help each & every young person grow personally & spiritually.
  • Training young people to lead the cause of christ, and apply Christianity to every area of life.
  • To reach out to the youth with the love of God as revealed to us in Jesus Christ Through the power of the Holy Spirit.
  • Provide a means for individuals to explore ideas, vales, anf Faith within the context of a supportive community of peers and adults.
  • Provide a variety of opportunities and experiences to draw teens into active, responsible participation in the life, mission, and work of the faith community and the greater church.
  • Help spiritually train and equip teens in a fun and Godly atmosphere to the Glory of God.
  • Serve as an umbrella term that describes the systematic attention the faith community gives to young people which enables them to reach their full God created potential. That is, everything the faith community is involved in that promotes both healthy development and faith growth in adolescents.
  • Works to foster the total personal and spiritual growth of each young person and seeks to draw young people into responsible participation in the life, mission and work of the faith community.
  • Teens Church is defined as the response of the Church to the needs of young people, fostering their faith in Jesus Christ and their communion with the Blessed Trinity, and drawing them into responsible participation in the life, mission and ministry of the Church.
  • Foster Spiritual development
  • Help young people become Christ followers; disciple them to be mature Christians. We do this by encouraging and empowering young people that are already Christ followers to live out their faith and share the love of Christ with their peers.

    • The Teens Church engages our teenagers in numerous and regular programs and activities ranging from embarking on indoor and outdoor adventures, exhibitions, games and competitions in recreational, sporting and mentally challenging exercises and ministering the Word of Faith to the teenagers, choreography, drama and other spiritually creative ministrations. The Teens Church comprises of teenagers those who are aged 13- 18 years and on attaining the age limit, they graduate into the Youth & Young Adult Fellowship.
